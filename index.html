The Story

Every Room Hides a Secret

Cursed Archive is a dark hidden-object puzzle game set inside a crumbling estate sealed for centuries. Each chamber is layered with shadows, relics, and riddles — and somewhere among the clutter, the answers wait.

Move through candlelit studies, dust-choked libraries, and vaulted crypts. Sharpen your eye, piece together the lore, and break the curse before it claims you.

  • 🕯️
    Atmospheric Scenes Hand-crafted environments dripping with detail and hidden depth.
  • 🔍
    Sharp-Eye Puzzles Dozens of cunningly concealed objects across ever-darkening rooms.
  • 📜
    Unfolding Mystery Collect clues and piece together the archive's forbidden history.
  • 🖥️
    Cross-Platform Available on Windows desktop and Android — play anywhere.
