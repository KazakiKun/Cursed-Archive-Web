The Story

Every Room Hides a Secret

Cursed Archive is a horror mystery game that combines investigation, puzzle-solving, and education into a unique experience. Players take on the role of an investigator exploring strange and unsettling cases hidden within a mysterious archive.

What makes Cursed Archive unique is its educational aspect. After completing a case, players are introduced to real-world laws and legal concepts related to the events they investigated — allowing you to learn while enjoying a thrilling horror experience.

  • 🕯️
    Investigate Comb through eerie scenes for clues that whisper the truth.
  • 🔍
    Solve Riddles and ciphers unlock evidence buried in the archive.
  • 📜
    Learn Each case ends with real-world law tied to what you uncovered.
